# Product Card

## Problem Statement

Inventory Coordinators need a quick way to understand why an individual product needs attention. Spreadsheet rows often require manual comparison of quantity, reorder thresholds, sales rates, and expiration dates.

## Solution Description

The StockWatch product card presents the important inventory facts together with the product’s alert category and a recommended next action. It gives the Inventory Coordinator enough context to decide what to do through the company’s existing process.

## Core Feature

Each product card should display:

- Product name, category, and brand
- Quantity on hand
- Reorder threshold and reorder quantity
- Expiration date
- Sales rate per day
- Storage condition
- Alert category or priority
- Recommended action
- Reviewed status

## MVP Boundary

The product card is display-only. StockWatch may calculate a status and recommend an action, but it does not place orders, contact suppliers, generate reports, or connect to a live inventory system.
